Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does Pureblaze Academy offer?

We offer a variety of programs including Information Technology, Computer Science, and Computer Engineering, among others.

How can I apply to Pureblaze Academy?

To apply, you need to visit our campus and submit your application in person. We do not accept online applications as we offer only face-to-face courses.

What is the application deadline?

Application deadlines vary by program. Please consult our Admissions page for specific dates and deadlines.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

Yes, we encourage prospective students to visit our campus.

What support services are available for students?

We offer a range of support services including academic advising, career counseling, and mental health resources.

Are there extracurricular activities available?

Yes, Pureblaze Academy offers a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, clubs, and student organizations.

What career services are available to students?

Our Career Services Office provides job search assistance, resume building workshops, interview preparation, and networking opportunities.

How can I contact the admissions office?

You can contact our admissions office via email at admissions@pureblaze.edu.ph or by phone at (+63) 9299764768.
